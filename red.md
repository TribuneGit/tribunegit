## The Pattern

**Institutional friction:** value already earned, trapped between an organization's operations and another institution's automated systems. A payer's AI denies a claim in 1.2 seconds. A construction subcontractor misses a lien deadline because the owner lookup took a week. A sales firm's methodology breaks down when a new analyst handles the account. The tools to counter all of these exist. Tribune builds the counter-system.

**Jeff's half:** End-to-end pipeline engineering + adversarial Senate review. Models from multiple independent providers attack every architecture before commitment.

**Engagement structure:** one Orchestrator as quality gate, two to four domain agents mapped to what requires judgment, one-way decisions held by named humans, ends with the Independence Test — five business days, real operations, zero Tribune intervention, mechanical pass or fail.
