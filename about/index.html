About

Built to be handed over.

Tribune was founded by two builders, Ryan Poole and Jeff Reinhold, who run their own operations on the same discipline they sell. We work from West Palm Beach, Florida, and work with businesses across the United States. We are not a software vendor and not a consultancy that never leaves. We build, we train, we test, we hand off.

The name is Roman: a tribune held office for one purpose, to strengthen the people he served, not to rule them. Our mark tells the same story: four stones hold a space shaped like a T. The T is never drawn. Tribune is the part that leaves, and the structure holds together around the space where we stood.

We win when you no longer need us.

Power that stays. Help that leaves.

Tribune Inc. builds complete AI organizations for small and mid-size businesses, designed around each company's costliest constraint, installed on hardware the client owns, and trained until they pass a formal Independence Test. Then Tribune hands over the keys and leaves. Tribune Inc. is a Delaware corporation. TribuneInc.com.
