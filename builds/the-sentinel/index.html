A Tribune build

The deadline that never surprises you again.

Licenses, credentials, renewals, filings, payer windows. The Sentinel tracks every clock in your business and raises its hand early, loudly, and to the right person.

The problem
  • Renewal notices arrive addressed to whoever left last year.
  • The filing window closed on money worth chasing.
  • Every deadline lives in someone's head.
A morning with your agent · illustrative

9:00 AM, the first Monday of the month. Everything expiring in the next ninety days, each with a named owner, the paperwork attached, and the date it becomes urgent. The one nobody acknowledged last week is now on the owner's desk, twice as tall.

What it does
  • Tracks licenses, credentials, renewals, filings, and payer windows in one place
  • Raises each deadline early, to a named owner, with what is needed
  • Escalates unacknowledged deadlines up the chain
  • Attaches the paperwork so the task starts ready
  • Logs completion for audits
  • Never lets a clock run silent

What this agent will never do

  • It will not take an irreversible action without a named human's sign-off.
  • It will not spend a cent without approval.
  • It will not send anything outside your walls without confirmation, to a customer, a payer, a court, or anyone else.
  • It will not guess. When it is unsure, it says so and asks the right person.
  • It will not police or blame your people. When something fails, we fix the system, not the person.
  • It will not move your data anywhere it does not belong.
Written purpose

I exist so the deadline that could hurt this business is never a surprise.

How it fits your team

A build is a small team, not a single tool: one lead agent your staff talks to, and two to four specialists behind it, each with a narrow job and a written purpose. Runs in your accounts, on infrastructure you own, with every action logged and reviewable. How we handle trust and compliance →

Questions owners ask

Who owns this system?

You do. Hardware, accounts, keys, documentation. Our access ends at handoff, and the handoff is gated by a formal Independence Test your team passes without us.

What happens to my team?

They get promoted by it. The training ladder ends with your people operating the system, and the recovery test is passed by your designated operator, unaided. We build agents that carry the repeatable work so your people can do the work only people can do.

Which deadlines can it track?

Anything with a date and an owner: regulatory, contractual, clinical, financial. The list is built in discovery from what has actually bitten you before.
