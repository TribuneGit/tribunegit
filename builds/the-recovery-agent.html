RECOVER MONEY OWED

Money you earned. Recovered before lunch.

Every day, revenue leaks through denials, rejections, and invoices nobody had time to chase. The Recovery Agent chases all of it, and brings your team a signed-off plan instead of a backlog.

Map your constraint

The constraint

  • Denied claims sit because everyone is busy.
  • Deadlines pass and recoverable money becomes unrecoverable.
  • You find out at month end, when it is too late.

A morning with your agent

Illustrative. 7:02 AM. Before your coffee, your agent has read every overnight remittance, sorted what is recoverable from what is not, drafted the corrections, attached each filing deadline, and queued the lot for your biller's sign-off. Nothing moves without a human. Nothing waits on one either.

What it does

  • Reads remittances and payment files
  • Classifies every denial by reason code
  • Separates recoverable from write-off
  • Drafts corrections and appeals
  • Attaches the deadline to every task
  • Posts clean status to your morning brief
  • Escalates anything unfamiliar to a human, with what it knows

What this agent will never do.

  • It will not take an irreversible action without a named human's sign-off.
  • It will not spend a cent without approval.
  • It will not send anything to a payer, a customer, or a court without confirmation.
  • It will not guess. When it is unsure, it says so and asks the right person.
  • It will not police or blame your people. When something fails, we fix the system, not the person.
  • It will not move your data anywhere it does not belong.

The soul card

"I exist to recover the revenue this practice earns but loses to friction, and to give the billing team their afternoons back."
