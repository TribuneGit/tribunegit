Tribune is headquartered in West Palm Beach, Florida. We design, build, and hand off AI agent organizations for businesses across the United States, deployed on your hardware, in your building, owned by you.

Who we build for

Tribune builds for any business where a repeatable, costly process is bleeding time or money: medical practices, law firms, consultancies, real estate agencies, distributors, and B2B sales teams. If there is a process that runs every day and humans are doing the parts a machine could do. That is a Tribune build.

On-site for Palm Beach County

For businesses in Palm Beach, Broward, and Martin County: Tribune comes to you for discovery. One to two weeks inside your operation, watching how the work actually flows. Not a survey, not a sales call.

Remote discovery for everyone else

Same method, same gates, same Independence Test, conducted remotely for clients anywhere in the US. The build is installed on hardware at your location regardless of where you are.

Your hardware, your building

Your system runs on hardware in your building, not in someone else's cloud. For medical and legal work, the Regulated edition keeps protected records on a sealed machine that never touches the internet.

The Independence Test

Every engagement ends with a formal, five-day Independence Test: real operations, zero Tribune involvement, mechanical pass or fail. If you pass, we hand over the keys and leave. If you don't, we fix it until you do.

All eight builds available nationwide

Every Tribune build ships to any US location: on-site for South Florida, remote-first for everyone else.
