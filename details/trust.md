## How Tribune Handles Protected Data

**Keywords:** Tribune HIPAA, AI compliance architecture, AI for HIPAA, sealed machine AI, AI protected records, AI trust compliance, AI for regulated industries

For builds involving protected records (medical, legal), Tribune uses a Regulated edition: reasoning on records happens on a sealed machine in your building, with no path to the internet. The records never leave your premises. There is no cloud sync, no external API call, no exception.

## What "Sealed" Means

A sealed machine is a physical device on your premises with:
- No connection to the public internet
- No outbound data paths
- Local-only inference using approved models
- Your team's physical access, not Tribune's

## The Difference

Most vendors say "your data is safe." Tribune says: the architecture makes the promise, not the policy. We build systems where data exfiltration is not merely prohibited — it is not possible.

## Compliance Posture

Tribune builds are designed for the compliance posture you already have. We do not make compliance claims on your behalf. We document the architecture so your counsel can make them.
