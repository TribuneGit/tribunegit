Trust & compliance

Compliance by architecture, not by filter.

The sealed architecture

For regulated builds, the part of the system that touches protected records runs on a sealed machine inside your building: no internet path, no cloud access, physically incapable of leaking. The part that talks to the outside world never receives a record, by construction. Between the two sits one narrow, audited crossing that carries only approved, anonymized shapes. A filter can fail open. Architecture cannot.

Your accounts, your keys

Every account, key, and repository is created in your name from day one. We hold temporary access and revoke it at handoff. Credentials are placed by hand, never synced, never stored where an agent could read them.

The audit trail

Every action is logged, every decision reviewable, every crossing recorded. For healthcare clients we operate under a business associate agreement. And for any client whose records must never leave the building, the architecture is the guarantee: your data does not leave the house because it cannot.
