FAQ

Questions owners actually ask.

Who owns the system?

You do. Accounts, keys, hardware, documentation. Our access ends at handoff.

What happens when something breaks after you leave?

Your operator has a manual that covers restart, logs, and recovery, and passed a live recovery test before we left. If you want us on call anyway, there is an optional service plan. Optional is the operative word.

Do your agents replace people?

We build them to carry the repeatable work so your people can do the work only people can do. We measure success in returned hours, and in systems staff are glad to work alongside. If you run the office: the handoff promotes you. You end the engagement operating the machine.

Which tools do you work with?

The ones you already use. We deploy into your existing stack; moving platforms is how AI projects die, so we do not ask you to.

How long does it take?

Typically six to ten weeks from discovery to handoff, gated by capability, not the calendar.

What does it cost?

Scoped per engagement in discovery. A discovery deposit starts the work and is credited toward your build.

Is Tribune only for medical practices?

No. The Regulated edition exists for businesses with protected records — medical, legal — because they are the hardest case, and we built for the hardest case first. The same builds run in their Standard edition for any business: sales teams, agencies, distributors, consultancies, service firms. Whatever is bleeding your business, start there.
