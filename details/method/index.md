## The Five Phases

**Keywords:** Tribune AI build process, how to build AI agent, AI build methodology, AI agent five phases, AI independence test process, AI build handover

**Phase 1 — Discovery:** Tribune maps the single costliest constraint in your operation. One to two weeks inside your business, ending in a report you confirm. A discovery deposit is required to begin.

**Phase 2 — Architecture:** Tribune designs the agent organization — which builds, which functions, which data sources — and presents it for your sign-off before writing a line of code.

**Phase 3 — Build:** Tribune builds on a machine in your building. Accounts, repos, and credentials are in your name from day one.

**Phase 4 — Training:** Your team and the agent run real work together. Tribune watches, corrects, and builds the training record your team will use to operate the system independently.

**Phase 5 — The Independence Test and Handoff:** Five business days. Tribune is unreachable. Your designated operator runs the system alone. Mechanical pass or fail — there is no conditional pass, and we do not soften the test to hit a date. On pass: Tribune hands over all keys, documentation, and access ends.

## Two Editions

**Standard Edition:** For businesses without regulated records. Deployed on your hardware or cloud, full capability, no sealed machine required.

**Regulated Edition:** For businesses with protected records (medical, legal). Reasoning on protected data happens on a sealed machine on your premises, with no path to the internet.
